Other Important Instructions

  1. The Question Paper consists of objective type questions as follows:
    SR.NO. Subjects No. of Questions
    CBT-1    		 Marks for each Section
    CBT-1
    1 General Subjects
    Total
  2. You will be given minutes to attempt questions. (Extra Minutes for eligible PwBD candidates accompanied with Scribe)
  3. Questions will be available in 15 languages.
  4. Each question will have 4 alternatives, out of which only one will be the correct answer.
  5. Candidates shall be awarded 1 mark for each correct answer. No mark shall be awarded or deducted for questions not attempted.
  6. There will be a negative marking of 1/3 mark per wrong answer attempted.
  7. One question will be displayed on the screen at a time. You are requested to be aware of the amount of time you spend per question as you have to answer questions in minutes.
  8. Copy the declaration displayed on your screen in your running Handwriting, (Not in Block Letters), only in the presence of invigilator on examination day. The candidature of the candidate shall be liable to be rejected if the candidate either writes this Mandatory Handwriting sample in Capital Letter or Leaves this space blank.
  9. Ensure that your passport size photograph is pasted on the RRB-foil of the e-call letter.
  10. During examination, your photograph and biometric will be captured.
  11. Bio break/ movement out of the lab is not permitted after the biometric LTI capture and photo capture.
  12. Candidates availing assistance of scribe will have to communicate with the scribe only in the language opted in the online application.
  13. Indulging in any type of malpractice/ unfair means/ misbehaviour/ indiscipline in the examination hall/ impersonation will disqualify the candidate and he/ she will be debarred from all Future RRB examinations & appointment in Railways.
  14. Candidate should write his/ her Name and Roll Number on the Rough sheet(s) provided for rough work. The Rough sheet(s) will have to be returned after the test.
